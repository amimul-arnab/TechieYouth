About Us

We are Techie Youth, a non-profit organization that helps foster children and in danger & at-risk youth children to develop them professionals to thrive in modern day world. Techie Youth focuses mainly on at-risk youth populations who are facing extreme life-challenges and those who are in guidance and assistance to learn how to become financially independent. Many students in our organization were statistically likely to become homeless or incarcerated. Knowing those odds, we have helped thousands of students alter their life to become independent adults with our services. With over 3,000+ students with locations throughout NYC including Long Island City, Bushwick, Brooklyn and locations in Long Island, techie youth provide an urban workplace for the youth and many of the students are seen on TV such as Fox 5, Pix 11 and abc channel for their outstanding work.
